Make Educational Apps for Students on the Go

Apps for Aptitude has developed an educational flashcard application. Students can easily access the flashcards posted on Quizlet.com made by teachers and other students. This application will be our major source of revenue which will be donated to programs fighting illiteracy in San Antonio.

Fight Illiteracy in the Greater San Antonio Area

We recognize that San Antonio has a literacy crisis with nearly 25% of the population illiterate or functionally illiterate. That is why we are planning to team up with organizations in the San Antonio area to lower the illiteracy rate in San Antonio.


School's Out Hackathon

Apps for Aptitude is planning something big...REALLY BIG. The School's Out Hackathon to be held from June 13th-14th at Rackspace in San Antonio, Texas. Join hundreds of high schoolers as they create in the largest hackathon of its kind. Programmers and beginners are welcome and best of all, it's free. Join us for cash prizes, free t-shirts, lectures, and workshops at our hackathon.

Cards for A Cause

One of the major problems facing students today is a way to have study materials available on the go. Apps for Aptitude has a solution.

We wanted to make sure that users could have access to a multitude of flashcards that will range from advanced mathematics to basic English concepts to SAT vocabulary guides. Cards for a Cause brings millions of flashcards to your mobile device with the simple touch of a button. It's Simple, Clean, and Efficient.

The revenue generated from this application will be donated to programs fighting illiteracy in San Antonio, increasing education for everyone.

School's Out Hackathon was a Success!

On June 13-14, 2014, Apps for Aptitude hosted the largest high school hackathon in the south with over 512 students in attendance. Over the course of 36 hours, students created a collective 80 apps, websites, and hardware hacks. Over $50,000 was raised for the event and was free to attend for all high schoolers.

A Little About Us

The purpose of Apps for Aptitude is relatively simple: we create educational games and apps that serve to increase scholarship. The idea behind this is that our education content should be available to everyone. We recognize that one of the root problems of illiteracy is lack of monetary funds, so we are committed to making sure that our educational content is available to everyone.
